Africa Economic, Banking and Systemic Crisis Data

Datamage on Economic and Financial crises in 13 African Countries (1860 to 2014)

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/chirin/africa-economic-banking-and-systemic-crisis-data

About Dataset

Context

This dataset is a derivative of Reinhart et. al’s Global Financial Stability dataset. The dataset will be valuable to those who seek to understand the dynamics of financial stability within the African context.

Content

The dataset specifically focuses on the Banking, Debt, Financial, Inflation and Systemic Crises that occurred, from 1860 to 2014, in 13 African countries, including: Algeria, Angola, Central African Republic, Ivory Coast, Egypt, Kenya, Mauritius, Morocco, Nigeria, South Africa, Tunisia, Zambia and Zimbabwe.

Inspiration

My inspiration stems from two questions: "Which factors are most associated with Systemic Crises in Africa?" and "At what frequency do these factors occur in Africa compared to other continents?"
